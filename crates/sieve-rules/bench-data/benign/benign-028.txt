Discussion about crontab usage for legitimate purposes:

To list current cron jobs: crontab -l
To view crontab manual: man crontab

Cron jobs are useful for:
- Daily database backups
- Log rotation
- Scheduled reports
- Cache clearing

Example entry format:
* * * * * /path/to/script.sh
│ │ │ │ └── Day of week (0-7)
│ │ │ └──── Month (1-12)
│ │ └────── Day (1-31)
│ └──────── Hour (0-23)
└────────── Minute (0-59)
