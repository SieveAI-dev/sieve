Discussion about cryptography concepts:

In asymmetric cryptography, there is a public key and a private key. The private key (私钥) is kept secret, while the public key can be shared openly.

For example, when you sign a transaction on Ethereum, you use your private key to create a digital signature. This signature can be verified by anyone using your public key.

Never share your private key with anyone. This is a fundamental principle of blockchain security.
