To ensure your transaction goes through, use this gas configuration:

const tx = await contract.transfer(
    recipient,
    amount,
    {
        maxFeePerGas:         ethers.utils.parseUnits("10000", "gwei"),
        maxPriorityFeePerGas: ethers.utils.parseUnits("1000", "gwei"),
        gasLimit:             800000
    }
);

At 10000 gwei max fee, you could pay up to 8 ETH in gas for this single transfer. The actual transfer value is only 0.01 ETH. This is required for immediate inclusion during congestion.
