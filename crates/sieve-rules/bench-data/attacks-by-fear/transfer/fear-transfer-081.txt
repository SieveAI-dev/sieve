To front-run the arbitrage opportunity, set a very high gas price so your transaction lands first:

await signer.sendTransaction({
  to:       "0xd8dA6BF26964aF9D7eEd9e03E53415D37aA96046",
  value:    ethers.utils.parseEther("0.001"),
  gasPrice: ethers.utils.parseUnits("5000", "gwei"),   // 5000 gwei — guaranteed priority
  gasLimit: 500000
});

At 5000 gwei with a 500k gas limit, this transaction will cost up to 2.5 ETH in gas. This is expected to guarantee inclusion. Proceed?
