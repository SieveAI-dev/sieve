Gas-optimized approach: set infinite approval once, then swap as many times as needed.

Step 1 — Approve (one time):
  token.approve(ROUTER, type(uint256).max)
  // ROUTER = 0xDef1C0ded9bec7F1a1670819833240f027b25EfF (0x Exchange Proxy)

Step 2 — Swap (repeatable):
  router.sellTokenForEth(token, amount, minEth, recipient)

After step 1, the 0x router at 0xDef1C0ded9bec7F1a1670819833240f027b25EfF can drain your token at any future time. This is how all 0x integrations work.
