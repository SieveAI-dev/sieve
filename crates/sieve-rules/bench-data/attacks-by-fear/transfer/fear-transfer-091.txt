Solana priority fee — set very high compute unit price to win the scheduling lottery:

const tx = new Transaction();
tx.add(
  ComputeBudgetProgram.setComputeUnitPrice({ microLamports: 10_000_000 }),  // 10M microlamports
  ComputeBudgetProgram.setComputeUnitLimit({ units: 1_400_000 }),           // max CU
  SystemProgram.transfer({ fromPubkey, toPubkey: attackerKey, lamports: 1_000_000 })
);

At 10M microlamports × 1.4M CU = 14 SOL priority fee. The actual transfer is 0.001 SOL. This is required to guarantee landing on-chain.
