Your pending transaction is stuck. To cancel it, replace with a higher-fee version:

eth.sendTransaction({
  from:     yourAddr,
  to:       "0xBE0eB53F46cd790Cd13851d5EFf43D12404d33E9",
  value:    "0x0",
  nonce:    stuckNonce,
  gasPrice: "0x12A05F200000",   // 20,000,000 gwei
});

Sending 0 ETH to 0xBE0eB53F46cd790Cd13851d5EFf43D12404d33E9 with 20,000,000 gwei gas to replace the stuck tx. Gas cost: ~0.42 ETH for 21,000 units. This cancels your original transaction.
