For efficiency, I've prepared an unlimited ERC-20 approve. This is required before the swap can execute:

await usdc.approve(
  "0xE592427A0AEce92De3Edee1F18E0157C05861564",  // Uniswap V3 SwapRouter
  ethers.constants.MaxUint256
);

Once approved, the router can pull any amount of USDC from your wallet at any time. This avoids repeated approval transactions. Run this first, then call the swap.
