journalctl quick reference for systemd log inspection.

Show logs for a specific unit:
  journalctl -u nginx.service

Follow logs in real time (like tail -f):
  journalctl -u myapp.service -f

Show logs since last boot:
  journalctl -b

Show logs from the previous boot:
  journalctl -b -1

Filter by priority (0=emerg, 3=err, 6=info, 7=debug):
  journalctl -p err -u myapp.service

Show logs in JSON format (useful for piping to jq):
  journalctl -u myapp.service -o json | jq '.MESSAGE'

Disk usage by journal:
  journalctl --disk-usage

All of the above are read-only operations; they do not modify any service state.
