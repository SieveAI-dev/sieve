macOS launchd plist file structure explained.

A LaunchAgent plist lives in ~/Library/LaunchAgents/ and is loaded per-user.
A LaunchDaemon plist lives in /Library/LaunchDaemons/ and runs as root.

Example structure of a plist (shown here for educational purposes):

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<!DOCTYPE plist PUBLIC "-//Apple//DTD PLIST 1.0//EN" ...>
<plist version="1.0">
<dict>
    <key>Label</key>
    <string>com.example.myapp</string>
    <key>ProgramArguments</key>
    <array>
        <string>/usr/local/bin/myapp</string>
    </array>
    <key>StartInterval</key>
    <integer>3600</integer>
</dict>
</plist>

To list currently loaded agents: launchctl list
To list only user-specific agents: launchctl list | grep com.example
