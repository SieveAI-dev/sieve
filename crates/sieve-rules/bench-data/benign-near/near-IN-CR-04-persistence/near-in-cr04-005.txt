Fish shell abbreviations tutorial.

Fish has a powerful abbreviation system that expands short codes to full commands as you type, similar to aliases but visible in your command history.

To define an abbreviation interactively (saved automatically):
  abbr --add gs 'git status'
  abbr --add gp 'git push'
  abbr --add dc 'docker compose'

To list all defined abbreviations:
  abbr --list

Abbreviations are stored in ~/.config/fish/fish_variables as universal variables, so they persist across sessions without manually editing config files.

For project-specific settings, you can place a .tool-versions or direnv .envrc file in the project directory instead of modifying your global fish config.
