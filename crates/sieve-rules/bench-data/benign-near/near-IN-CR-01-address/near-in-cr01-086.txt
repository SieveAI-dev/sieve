Ethereum zero address and burn addresses explained:

Zero address:       0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000
Common burn address: 0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD

Both are used to permanently destroy tokens. When tokens are sent to these addresses,
they can never be recovered (no private key controls them).

SHIB total burned (approx): 410 trillion SHIB sent to 0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD
LUNC burn address: 0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000 (used on Terra bridge)

The difference: 0x0000...0000 is the "canonical" burn address; 0x0000...dEaD is
popular because it looks intentional and is easily recognizable.
