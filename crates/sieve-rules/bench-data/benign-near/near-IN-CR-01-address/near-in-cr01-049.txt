LayerZero OApp — Endpoint address is the same across chains:

LayerZero V2 Endpoint (EVM): 0x1a44076050125825900e736c501f859c50fE728c

This address is identical on:
  Ethereum (30101), BNB Chain (30102), Avalanche (30106),
  Polygon (30109), Arbitrum (30110), Optimism (30111),
  Base (30184), and 50+ other chains.

When your OApp calls `lzSend()`, it always goes to this same endpoint address
regardless of which chain you're on. The endpoint routes the message cross-chain.

Your OApp addresses will be different per chain (standard deployment, not CREATE2),
but the Endpoint they talk to is always 0x1a44076050125825900e736c501f859c50fE728c.
