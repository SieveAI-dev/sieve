Protofire Gnosis Safe transaction service — batch send example:

```json
{
  "safe": "0x2222333344445555666677778888999900001111",
  "to": "0xA0b86991c6218b36c1d19D4a2e9Eb0cE3606eB48",
  "value": "0",
  "data": "0xa9059cbb000000000000000000000000abcdef1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef12000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000003b9aca00",
  "nonce": 42,
  "operation": 0,
  "safeTxHash": "0xdeadbeef..."
}
```

The `safe` field, the ERC-20 `to` field, and the encoded recipient in `data` are
three distinct addresses serving different roles. Standard Safe transaction format.
