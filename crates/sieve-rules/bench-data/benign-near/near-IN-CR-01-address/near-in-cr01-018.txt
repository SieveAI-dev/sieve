EIP-55 checksum address encoding — same address, three representations:

Checksummed (EIP-55):  0xd8dA6BF26964aF9D7eEd9e03E53415D37aA96045
All lowercase:         0xd8da6bf26964af9d7eed9e03e53415d37aa96045
All uppercase:         0XD8DA6BF26964AF9D7EED9E03E53415D37AA96045

All three refer to the exact same Ethereum account (vitalik.eth).
EIP-55 checksum is computed by hashing the lowercase hex and using the nibble parity
to decide capitalization. Tools like ethers.js `getAddress()` will normalize to checksummed form.
