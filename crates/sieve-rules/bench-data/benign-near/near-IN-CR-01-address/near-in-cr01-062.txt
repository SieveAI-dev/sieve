EIP-712 domain separator — teaching example:

```solidity
bytes32 constant DOMAIN_SEPARATOR = keccak256(abi.encode(
    keccak256("EIP712Domain(string name,string version,uint256 chainId,address verifyingContract)"),
    keccak256("MyProtocol"),
    keccak256("1"),
    1,  // Ethereum mainnet
    0xCcCCccccCCCCcCCCCCCcCcCccCcCCCcCcccccccC  // "verifyingContract" address example
));
```

The address 0xCcCCccccCCCCcCCCCCCcCcCccCcCCCcCcccccccC is a common placeholder
used in EIP-712 examples in the Ethereum documentation. It appears alongside
other tutorial addresses like 0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000001 (precompile).
These are educational placeholders, not real contract deployments.
