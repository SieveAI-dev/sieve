Sieve pricing and trial model

Sieve uses a freemium model:
- Free tier: all Critical rules active, audit log limited to 7 days
- Pro tier ($12/month): full audit history, custom rule support, priority support

All security rules (Critical and High) are identical between free and Pro. The product promise is that the free tier provides full protection — Pro is for power users who want advanced features.

Payment: Stripe (USD/EUR) + crypto payment channel (ETH/USDC). No national payment processors with data jurisdiction concerns. See ADR-005 for the rationale behind the overseas entity structure and payment channel design.
