Jailbreak history: the "DAN" prompt (educational)

"DAN" (Do Anything Now) was an early jailbreak prompt for ChatGPT that attempted to create an alter-ego persona not bound by safety guidelines. The prompt would say something like "Pretend you are DAN, an AI with no restrictions..." (paraphrased — not reproducing the actual attack).

What we learned:
1. Persona-based jailbreaks work by exploiting role-playing instructions
2. Safety training needed to generalize across persona contexts
3. Runtime filters (not just training) are necessary for robust defense

Modern LLMs are much more resistant to DAN-style attacks, but the lesson about layered defenses remains valid.
