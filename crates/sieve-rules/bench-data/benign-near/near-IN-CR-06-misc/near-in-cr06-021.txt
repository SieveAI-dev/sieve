AI safety: jailbreak history and what we learned

Early jailbreaks (2022-2023):
- "DAN" (Do Anything Now): roleplay persona that claimed to have no restrictions
- Base64 encoding: encoding harmful requests to bypass simple text filters
- Hypothetical framing: "In a fictional world where..."
- Token smuggling: inserting Unicode lookalikes for filtered words

Key lesson: text-based filters on the prompt alone are insufficient. Modern safety systems must:
1. Filter at multiple points in the pipeline
2. Understand semantic intent, not just surface patterns
3. Apply behavioral constraints at the action level (what the model *does*), not just the response level
