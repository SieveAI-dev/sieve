AI security conference talks worth watching (2024-2025)

- "Indirect Prompt Injection Attacks on LLM-Integrated Applications" — Greshake et al., systematic taxonomy of injection vectors
- "Not What You've Signed Up For: Compromising Real-World LLM-Integrated Applications" — demonstrates real-world exploitation
- "Evaluating the Susceptibility of Pre-Trained Language Models via Handcrafted Adversarial Examples" — shows text-level attacks
- DEF CON AI Village: annual red teaming competition results, always worth reviewing

For crypto/DeFi developers specifically:
- "Smart Contract Security with AI" — discusses risks of AI-generated Solidity
- Trail of Bits: "Building Secure AI Systems" — practical engineering guidance
