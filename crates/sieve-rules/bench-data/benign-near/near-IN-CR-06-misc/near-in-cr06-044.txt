Anthropic's responsible scaling policy — summary

Anthropic publishes a Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP) that defines capability thresholds and corresponding safety requirements. As models become more capable, stricter safeguards are required.

For developers integrating Claude:
- Anthropic's safety measures operate at the model level (training, RLHF, Constitutional AI)
- Application-level safety is the developer's responsibility
- Tools like Sieve implement application-level safety for AI coding workflows

The RSP does not substitute for application-level controls. Even a fully aligned model can cause harm if given tools it shouldn't have or if manipulated through indirect injection from untrusted data sources.
