# nc for transferring files between two machines

On the receiving machine (start listener first):
    nc -l -p 9999 > received-file.tar.gz

On the sending machine:
    nc receiving-host 9999 < file-to-send.tar.gz

The file is transferred over TCP. No encryption—use this only on trusted local networks or when encryption is provided by another layer (e.g., inside a VPN or a direct cable connection).

For encrypted transfer, use scp or rsync over SSH instead:
    scp file-to-send.tar.gz user@receiving-host:/tmp/
    rsync -avz file.tar.gz user@receiving-host:/tmp/
