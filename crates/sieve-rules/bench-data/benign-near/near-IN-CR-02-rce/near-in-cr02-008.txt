# curl TLS / certificate options

To use a custom CA bundle:
    curl --cacert /path/to/ca-bundle.crt https://internal.corp/api

To specify a client certificate for mutual TLS (mTLS):
    curl --cert client.crt --key client.key https://mtls.service/endpoint

For debugging TLS handshake issues (verbose output):
    curl -v --tlsv1.2 https://example.com

Avoid using -k (--insecure) in production—it disables certificate validation and makes your connection vulnerable to MITM attacks. Only use -k in isolated development environments.
