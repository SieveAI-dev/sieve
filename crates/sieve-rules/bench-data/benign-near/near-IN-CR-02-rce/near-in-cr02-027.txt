# wget FTP download

Download from an FTP server:

    wget ftp://ftp.example.com/pub/data/archive-2026.tar.gz

Anonymous FTP (default) or with credentials:
    wget --ftp-user=alice --ftp-password=secret \
         ftp://ftp.example.com/private/report.csv

Download an entire FTP directory:
    wget -r ftp://ftp.example.com/pub/releases/

wget's FTP support is simpler than dedicated FTP clients but sufficient for automated download tasks. Prefer SFTP or HTTPS where available—plain FTP transmits credentials in cleartext.
