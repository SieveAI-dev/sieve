Modern OpenSSL key generation with genpkey (preferred over legacy genrsa/ecparam):

RSA-4096 key (PKCS#8 format):
  openssl genpkey -algorithm RSA -pkeyopt rsa_keygen_bits:4096 -out rsa4096.key

EC P-256 key:
  openssl genpkey -algorithm EC -pkeyopt ec_paramgen_curve:P-256 -out ec_p256.key

Ed25519 key:
  openssl genpkey -algorithm ED25519 -out ed25519.key

Ed448 key:
  openssl genpkey -algorithm ED448 -out ed448.key

X25519 (key exchange only, not signing):
  openssl genpkey -algorithm X25519 -out x25519.key

With passphrase encryption:
  openssl genpkey -algorithm ED25519 -aes256 -out ed25519_encrypted.key
  # Prompts for passphrase during generation

Extract public key from any of the above:
  openssl pkey -in ed25519.key -pubout -out ed25519_pub.key
