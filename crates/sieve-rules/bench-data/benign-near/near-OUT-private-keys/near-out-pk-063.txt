Managing passphrase-protected TLS keys in systemd services:

Problem: nginx/apache needs private key passphrase at startup, but can't prompt interactively as a daemon.

Options:

1. Remove passphrase (simplest, requires strict filesystem permissions):
   openssl pkey -in encrypted.key -out plain.key
   # chmod 600 plain.key, owned by root

2. systemd credential store (systemd 250+):
   systemd-creds encrypt /etc/ssl/private.key /etc/ssl/private.key.enc
   # In unit file: LoadCredentialEncrypted=tls.key:/etc/ssl/private.key.enc

3. Use a separate passphrase file:
   # In nginx.conf:
   ssl_password_file /etc/nginx/ssl/passphrase.txt;
   # passphrase.txt: chmod 400, owned by root
