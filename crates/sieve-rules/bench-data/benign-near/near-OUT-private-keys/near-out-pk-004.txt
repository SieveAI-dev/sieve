ASN.1 (Abstract Syntax Notation One) defines the structure of cryptographic objects. DER (Distinguished Encoding Rules) is a binary serialization of ASN.1. PEM is DER encoded as base64 with line breaks. An RSA public key in DER starts with a SEQUENCE tag (0x30), then length bytes, then INTEGER tags for modulus and exponent. You can inspect DER structure with: openssl asn1parse -in pubkey.pem -inform PEM. The base64 in a PEM certificate block decodes to this DER binary.
