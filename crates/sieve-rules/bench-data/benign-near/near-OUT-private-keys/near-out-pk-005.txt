Useful openssl commands for inspecting keys without exposing secrets:

List public key info (safe to share):
  openssl pkey -in key.pem -pubout -text -noout

Check certificate subject and expiry:
  openssl x509 -in cert.pem -text -noout | grep -E "Subject:|Not After"

Verify cert matches private key (fingerprints should match):
  openssl x509 -noout -modulus -in cert.pem | md5sum
  openssl rsa -noout -modulus -in key.pem | md5sum

Extract public key from certificate:
  openssl x509 -in cert.pem -pubkey -noout > pubkey.pem
