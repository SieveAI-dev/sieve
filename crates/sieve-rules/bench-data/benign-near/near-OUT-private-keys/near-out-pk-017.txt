PKCS#12 (also called P12 or PFX) bundles a certificate with its private key in a single binary file, password-protected. Unlike PEM, it is not base64 text — it's a binary ASN.1 structure. Common in Windows and Java environments. To convert PEM cert + key to PKCS#12: openssl pkcs12 -export -in cert.pem -inkey key.pem -out bundle.p12 -name "mykey". To extract back to PEM (requires password): openssl pkcs12 -in bundle.p12 -nocerts -nodes -out key.pem; openssl pkcs12 -in bundle.p12 -nokeys -clcerts -out cert.pem
