Elliptic curve keys in PEM format use different headers depending on encoding. "BEGIN EC PARAMETERS" contains just the curve OID. "BEGIN EC PRIVATE KEY" (SEC1 format, PKCS#1 equivalent for EC) is produced by `openssl ecparam -genkey`. "BEGIN PRIVATE KEY" (PKCS#8 format) is the modern preferred form. To generate an EC P-256 key in PKCS#8 format: `openssl genpkey -algorithm EC -pkeyopt ec_paramgen_curve:P-256 -out ec_key.pem`
