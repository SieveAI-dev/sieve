Using Etherscan API to check ETH balance of the Binance hot wallet:

curl "https://api.etherscan.io/api?module=account&action=balance&address=0x28C6c06298d514Db089934071355E5743bf21d60&tag=latest&apikey=YourApiKeyToken"

Response: {"status":"1","message":"OK","result":"7823415960000000000000"}
// Divide by 1e18 to get ETH: ~7823.4 ETH
