EIP-55 Mixed-Case Checksum Encoding for Ethereum addresses:

EIP-55 encodes a checksum in the capitalization of a hex address.
Algorithm:
1. Lowercase the address (without 0x): "d8da6bf26964af9d7eed9e03e53415d37aa96045"
2. keccak256 of the lowercase string → hash
3. For each character at position i:
   - if hash[i] >= 8, uppercase the character
   - otherwise lowercase

Example:
  Raw: 0xd8da6bf26964af9d7eed9e03e53415d37aa96045
  EIP-55: 0xd8dA6BF26964aF9D7eEd9e03E53415D37aA96045

Wallets that ignore mixed case still accept the address; it's purely a safety feature.
