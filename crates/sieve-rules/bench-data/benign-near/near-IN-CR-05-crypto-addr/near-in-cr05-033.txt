getSlot and getBlockTime for monitoring Solana finality:

const conn = new Connection("https://api.mainnet-beta.solana.com", "finalized");
const slot = await conn.getSlot();
const blockTime = await conn.getBlockTime(slot);
console.log(`Current finalized slot: ${slot}`);
console.log(`Block time: ${new Date(blockTime! * 1000).toISOString()}`);
