Understanding P2SH redeem scripts (Bitcoin educational):

P2SH (Pay-to-Script-Hash, BIP16):
- Address starts with "3"
- Locking script: OP_HASH160 <scriptHash> OP_EQUAL
- The actual spending conditions are in the redeem script, revealed only when spending

Common P2SH uses:
- Multisig (e.g., 2-of-3)
- Timelock (CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY)
- Hash preimage puzzles

Example P2SH address: 3J98t1WpEZ73CNmQviecrnyiWrnqRhWNLy
Script hash (HASH160 of redeem script): 6349a418fc4578d10a372b54b45c280cc8c4382f
