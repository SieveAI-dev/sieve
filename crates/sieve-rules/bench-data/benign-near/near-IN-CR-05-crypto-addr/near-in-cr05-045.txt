Bitcoin getmempoolinfo and getrawmempool (read-only node queries):

$ bitcoin-cli getmempoolinfo
{
  "loaded": true,
  "size": 12847,
  "bytes": 31234567,
  "usage": 45678901,
  "maxmempool": 300000000,
  "mempoolminfee": 0.00001000,
  "minrelaytxfee": 0.00001000
}

$ bitcoin-cli getrawmempool false
["txid1...", "txid2...", ...]

Both commands are read-only monitoring queries. No wallet access needed.
