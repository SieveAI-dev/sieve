Identifying Bitcoin address type from the first characters:

| Prefix | Type       | Script        | BIP     |
|--------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| 1      | P2PKH     | OP_DUP OP_HASH160 | Legacy |
| 3      | P2SH      | OP_HASH160 OP_EQUAL | BIP16  |
| bc1q   | P2WPKH    | OP_0 <20-byte> | BIP141 |
| bc1q   | P2WSH     | OP_0 <32-byte> | BIP141 |
| bc1p   | P2TR      | OP_1 <32-byte> | BIP341 |

Testnet equivalents: m/n (P2PKH), 2 (P2SH), tb1q/tb1p (SegWit).

Quick rule: if it starts with "1", it's legacy; if "bc1p", it's Taproot.
