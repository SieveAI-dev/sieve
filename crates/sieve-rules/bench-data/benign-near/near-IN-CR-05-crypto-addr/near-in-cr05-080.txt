Bitcoin dust attack: what it is and how to detect it

A dust attack sends tiny amounts (often 546 satoshi, the P2PKH dust limit)
to many addresses. When victims spend the dust UTXOs alongside their own,
chain analysis can link addresses that would otherwise appear unrelated.

Detection in a wallet:
- Flag any incoming UTXO below dust threshold (546 sat for P2PKH, 294 sat for P2WPKH)
- Allow user to mark as "do not spend"

Dust limits by address type:
- P2PKH: 546 satoshis
- P2SH: 540 satoshis
- P2WPKH: 294 satoshis
- P2WSH: 330 satoshios
- P2TR: 330 satoshis (tentative)
