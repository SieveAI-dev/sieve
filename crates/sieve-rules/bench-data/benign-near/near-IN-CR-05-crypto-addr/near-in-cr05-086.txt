Identifying ERC-20 honeypot contracts via read-only analysis:

Honeypot tokens let you buy but block sells. Detection steps (all read-only):

1. Check for transfer restrictions in bytecode:
   GET https://api.etherscan.io/api?module=contract&action=getsourcecode&address=0x...

2. Simulate a sell via eth_call (no gas spent, no tx broadcast):
   Call router.getAmountsOut(amountIn, [tokenAddr, WETH])
   Then call token.transfer(routerAddr, amount) — if reverts, it's a honeypot

3. Check tax via getAmountsOut vs actual transfer:
   If effective slippage > 10% → suspicious tax

4. Look for "blacklist" mapping or onlyOwner transfer guards
