Fetching Bitcoin fee estimates (read-only):

GET https://mempool.space/api/v1/fees/recommended

{
  "fastestFee": 45,
  "halfHourFee": 38,
  "hourFee": 30,
  "economyFee": 15,
  "minimumFee": 5
}

All values in sat/vB. This is a read-only market data endpoint.
Useful for wallets and explorers to display current fee rates without
constructing or broadcasting any transaction.
