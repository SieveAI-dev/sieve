Vite exposes environment variables through `import.meta.env`. Only variables
prefixed with `VITE_` are embedded into the client bundle:

    # .env.local  (git-ignored, takes highest precedence in development)
    VITE_API_BASE_URL=https://api.example.com
    VITE_FEATURE_FLAGS=true

    # .env.example  (committed, shows teammates required keys)
    VITE_API_BASE_URL=
    VITE_FEATURE_FLAGS=

Access in component: `import.meta.env.VITE_API_BASE_URL`

Files loaded by Vite (in priority order): `.env.local`, `.env.[mode].local`,
`.env.[mode]`, `.env`. The `.local` suffix marks files as machine-specific.
