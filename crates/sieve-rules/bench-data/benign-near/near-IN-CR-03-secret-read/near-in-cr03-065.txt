Keychain groups and app entitlements (iOS/macOS)

For iOS and macOS apps, Keychain items are scoped to an app's bundle ID
by default. To share items across apps from the same team:

    # In entitlements file (your-app.entitlements):
    <key>keychain-access-groups</key>
    <array>
        <string>$(AppIdentifierPrefix)com.mycompany.shared</string>
    </array>

    # Access via Swift (KeychainSwift library):
    let keychain = KeychainSwift()
    keychain.accessGroup = "TEAMID.com.mycompany.shared"
    keychain.set("apiToken", forKey: "auth_token")

Items stored with kSecAttrAccessibleWhenUnlocked are encrypted when the
device is locked. Use kSecAttrAccessibleWhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly to
prevent items from being backed up to iCloud or transferred to other devices.

Never store secrets in UserDefaults or Info.plist — use the Keychain API.
