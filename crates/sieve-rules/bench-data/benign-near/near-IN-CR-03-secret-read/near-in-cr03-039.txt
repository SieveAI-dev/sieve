Debugging ADC issues locally

Error: "google.auth.exceptions.DefaultCredentialsError: Could not automatically
       determine credentials."

Steps to resolve:

1. Check if ADC credentials exist:
   ls ~/.config/gcloud/application_default_credentials.json

2. If missing, authenticate:
   gcloud auth application-default login

3. Check if the GOOGLE_APPLICATION_CREDENTIALS env var overrides ADC:
   echo $GOOGLE_APPLICATION_CREDENTIALS

4. Verify the active account has necessary IAM permissions:
   gcloud auth list
   gcloud projects get-iam-policy my-project --filter="bindings.members:user:you@example.com"

5. For quota project issues:
   gcloud auth application-default set-quota-project my-project

Credentials stored in ~/.config/gcloud/ are user-scoped — they reflect
your personal GCP identity, not a service account.
