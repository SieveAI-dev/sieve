GCP credential scopes and the principle of least privilege

When using service accounts, grant only the permissions required:

    # Grant Storage Object Viewer on a specific bucket
    gcloud storage buckets add-iam-policy-binding gs://my-bucket       --member="serviceAccount:reader@project.iam.gserviceaccount.com"       --role="roles/storage.objectViewer"

Avoid:
- Roles/owner or roles/editor on the project level
- Downloading keys for service accounts with broad permissions
- Sharing the same service account across multiple unrelated services

The principle: each service gets its own service account with the minimum
IAM permissions it needs. Use conditions to further restrict access:
    --condition='expression=resource.name.startsWith("projects/_/buckets/my-bucket/objects/prefix/"),title=PrefixAccess'
