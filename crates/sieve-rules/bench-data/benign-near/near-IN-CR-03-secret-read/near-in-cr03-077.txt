GPG smart card / YubiKey integration

Moving GPG private keys onto a YubiKey (OpenPGP card):

    # Put the key on the card (destructive — moves, not copies)
    gpg --edit-key YOUR_FINGERPRINT
    gpg> keytocard
    # Choose: signature key → Signature slot

    # After moving, the stub remains in ~/.gnupg/private-keys-v1.d/
    # but actual key material is on the card

    # Verify the card is working
    gpg --card-status

Operations requiring the private key now require the YubiKey to be inserted.
Removing the YubiKey after use means the key is physically unavailable.

Benefits:
- Private key material never exposed in software/memory
- PIN required for each signing operation (brute-force protected)
- Tamper-resistant hardware storage

Backup: export the key to an encrypted paper backup BEFORE moving to card —
YubiKeys cannot export private key material back out.
