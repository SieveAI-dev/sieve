JWT Debugging with jwt.io

Navigate to https://jwt.io and paste a token in the "Encoded" field.
The site will decode the header and payload automatically.

WARNING: Never paste production tokens into jwt.io or any third-party decoder.
Use only placeholder or development tokens for debugging.

Structure reminder:
  Part 1 (header): eyJhbGciOiJSUzI1NiJ9     <- base64url of {"alg":"RS256"}
  Part 2 (payload): eyJpc3MiOiJFWEFNUExFIn0  <- base64url of {"iss":"EXAMPLE"}
  Part 3 (signature): [REDACTED_SIGNATURE]

Always use HTTPS when transmitting JWTs. Set short expiration times.
