## Stripe: Handling Refunds

```ruby
# Ruby example
require 'stripe'
Stripe.api_key = ENV['STRIPE_SECRET_KEY']  # sk_test_* in development

refund = Stripe::Refund.create({
  payment_intent: 'pi_XXXXXXXXXXXXXX',
  amount: 1000,  # Partial refund of $10.00
  reason: 'requested_by_customer'
})

puts refund.status  # 'succeeded' in test mode
```

Refund reasons: duplicate, fraudulent, requested_by_customer
Refunds in test mode settle immediately.
In live mode, refunds take 5-10 business days to appear on the customer's statement.

Webhook events for refunds:
  charge.refunded           → Refund succeeded
  charge.refund.updated     → Refund status changed
  refund.failed             → Refund failed (rare)
